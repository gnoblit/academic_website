CULTURAL EVOLUTION, INSTITUTIONS, AND MINDS

I am a Post-Doctoral Scholar at the Schwartz Reisman Institute for Technology and Society at the University of Toronto and Vector Institute for Artificial Intelligence where I use game theory to understand how norms, culture, and political institutions evolve and how such insights can be applied in multi-agent reinforcement learning. I recently got my Ph.D. from the Culture, Cognition, and Coevolution Lab at Harvard University.
